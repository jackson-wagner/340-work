Nachos

This is a great meal for the 4th of July.
It also tastes good any other time.

Ingredients

  • chips
  • cheese
  • onions
  • salsa
  • ground beef
  • CO2

    • Just writing this makes me think of a great quote by Dr. Not Chose

    Each day is like a fresh bowl of nachos

    Sometimes I look at the images in Ultimate Nachos for inspiration.
    Eating nachos for breakfast is a horrible great idea.

    1. Cook the ground beef.
      • I usually do this in a skillet.
      • Make sure you add seasoning after the meat is brown.
    2. Put some chips on a plate.
    3. Sprinkle shredded cheese on the chips and microwave for 20 seconds.
    4. Chop the onion.
    5. Combine the meat, onions and salsa with the chips and cheese.
    6. Enjoy.

      7. There are also some other types of nachos, which you can find here.
